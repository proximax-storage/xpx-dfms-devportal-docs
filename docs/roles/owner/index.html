Drive Owner

Overview

The owner is a DFMS role that initiates files storage in the network. To create a new drive, the owner should prepare and create a new drive account.

What does the owner do?

  1. The owner creates a drive account and prepares it with desired properties. When the drive is prepared, the owner must transfer XPX to drive balance that equals at least one billing period.
  2. The owner can upload, remove, rename, move, download files and directories in drive. Every action has its price and must be paid by the owner.
  3. The owner can initiate verification of storing files by replicators (the Challenge). Each owner wants to save its file safe and secure.
  4. The owner makes payments to replicators. When the owner doesn't pay for the next billing period replicators can terminate the drive.
